//About

Flock Follow lets users share their live location with friends or within groups in real-time. Built with a robust Python (Django) backend and a sleek Flutter (Dart) mobile interface, powered by Google Maps APIs for accurate tracking. Play Store–policy compliant and deployable on Namecheap.
